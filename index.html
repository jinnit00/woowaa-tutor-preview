Why This Exists

This isn't tutoring. It's a weekly conversation between two worlds.

LAIBA exists because Taiwan's most curious people want more than language drills — and the international community here has stories that don't fit on a CV.

LAIBA 不是補習。它是讓臺灣最求知若渴的人，與在臺國際社群每週一次的真實對話。

Conversation, not curriculum

No textbooks. No grading. Just two interesting humans, once a week, in a Taiwanese home — talking about life, work, culture, ideas.

Selective on both sides

We vet members the way we vet teachers. That means time spent here is time well spent — for both sides.

From language to art, to architecture, to ideas

Language is our starting point. Future cohorts will expand into art, architecture, and other cultural disciplines.

Teacher Profile · 老師資格

Who can teach

  • Holders of Taiwan's Gold Card (employment-based foreign professional)
  • Visiting researchers and academics in Taiwan
  • Exchange or graduate students from top international universities
  • Mother-tongue speakers with verified cultural depth

Casual visitors and unverified backgrounds will not be accepted.

老師主要來自：在臺灣領有就業金卡的國際專業人士、來臺研究員、重點學校的大學生／交換生。嚴格排除外籍無業遊民。

Member Vetting · 會員審核

Who can join

  • Under 18: we vet the parents — career, profession, household
  • 18 and over: we vet the individual — career trajectory, professional record
  • Open mind, willingness to engage across cultures
  • Active learning attitude — show up, ask, listen

未滿 18 歲學員：審核家長。滿 18 歲以上青年：審核個人職涯與經歷。訴求：開放的心、接納不同文化、積極的學習態度。目的：打造良好的學習環境。

What a Session Looks Like

One conversation, once a week, at home.

No classroom, no homework. Here's what actually happens, step by step.

01

You arrive at a real family's home

Vetted address. Coffee or tea on the table. Your member is expecting you — often the whole family.

02

You talk about whatever matters

Last weekend's gallery opening. A book they're reading. Your hometown's cuisine. Their child's interest in space. There's no script.

03

90 minutes pass quickly

Most sessions run 60–90 minutes. Some longer when there's a meal. Some shorter when the kids have homework.

04

You leave a little richer — both ways

They've learned about your world. You've learned about theirs. Payment lands in your account by the end of the week.

FAQ

What people ask before applying.

Do I need teaching experience?

No. We're looking for cultural depth, professional credibility, and genuine curiosity — not teaching credentials. Members are not coming for grammar drills.

不需要教學經驗。我們看重文化深度、職涯可信度、與真誠的好奇心──會員不是為了文法練習而來。

How safe are home visits?

Every member household is vetted before being matched. Addresses are verified. You'll always know the family in advance — and you can decline any match at any time, no reason needed.

每個會員家庭都經過審核、地址核實。配對前你會先認識對方家庭，任何配對你都能拒絕，無需理由。

Is this compatible with my gold-card / research visa?

Yes. The compensation structure is designed to be compatible with employment gold cards and visiting researcher visas. We can clarify edge cases on the intro call.

薪資結構與就業金卡、研究簽證相容。複雜個案可在 15 分鐘 intro call 確認。

What if I'm a graduate student, not a professional?

Top-school graduate and exchange students are very welcome. Members specifically appreciate cross-generational matches — younger teachers often pair well with younger members.

頂尖學校的研究生／交換生很歡迎。會員特別欣賞跨世代配對，年輕老師常與年輕會員契合度高。

How long until I get matched?

Typically 1–3 weeks after the intro call, depending on member availability in your culture cluster. We won't rush a bad match.

通常 15 分鐘 intro call 後 1–3 週內配對，視該文化區會員空檔而定。我們不會急著做壞的配對。

Do I have to commit long-term?

No. You can pause anytime. Most teachers stay for at least a semester — but that's a function of fit, not a contractual obligation.

不需要長期承諾。任何時候皆可暫停。多數老師會自然待上一學期以上，但那是契合度的結果、不是合約義務。
